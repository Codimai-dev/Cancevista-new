Our Approach to Care

At Cancervistas, we believe cancer care must address the whole person – not just the disease. Our integrated services create a supportive ecosystem around each patient and family, bringing specialized care directly to you.

We understand that every cancer journey is unique. That's why our services can be accessed individually or as comprehensive care packages tailored to your specific situation.

For Patients and Families

Oncology Consultation

Speak with experienced cancer specialists without the burden of travel. Our oncologists bring years of specialized experience directly to you through secure video consultations.

What to expect:

  • In-depth review of your medical history and diagnosis
  • Clear explanation of your condition in language you can understand
  • Personalized treatment recommendations based on latest protocols
  • Opportunity to ask questions and address concerns
  • Written summary of consultation findings and next steps
  • Follow-up coordination with local healthcare providers

We offer consultations with medical oncologists, surgical oncologists, radiation oncologists, and other specialists based on your specific needs.

Book Your Consultation

Cancer Medicine Delivery

Cancer medications require perfect timing and careful handling. Our medication service ensures you receive the right medicines at the right time – delivered right to your door.

How it works:

  • Upload your prescription through our secure portal or app
  • Our pharmacist reviews your medication needs and verifies the prescription
  • Medications are sourced from authorized distributors with complete batch tracking
  • Temperature-sensitive medications are shipped in special cooling packaging
  • Regular refill reminders ensure you never miss a dose
  • Monthly subscription options available for ongoing treatments

All medications come with easy-to-understand information sheets explaining usage instructions, possible side effects, and when to contact your doctor.

Order Your Medications

Diagnostic Testing

Proper testing is crucial for diagnosis and monitoring treatment progress. We bring laboratory services closer to you, eliminating unnecessary travel for routine tests.

Our testing services include:

  • Home sample collection by trained phlebotomists
  • Access to essential diagnostic tests through our partner lab network
  • Clear, jargon-free explanation of your test results
  • Digital reports shared directly with your treating doctors
  • Longitudinal tracking to monitor changes over time
  • Specialized cancer marker tests and molecular diagnostics

Our care coordinators help schedule your tests at times convenient for you and ensure results reach your medical team promptly.

Schedule Your Tests

Nutrition Support

Good nutrition is a powerful ally during cancer treatment. Our oncology nutritionists help you maintain strength and well-being through personalized dietary guidance.

How we support your nutrition needs:

  • Initial assessment of your nutritional status and specific challenges
  • Customized meal plans that consider your treatment, preferences, and cultural background
  • Practical strategies for managing treatment-related eating difficulties
  • Family education on preparing appropriate meals
  • Regular follow-up to adjust recommendations as needs change
  • Guidance on supplements when appropriate

Our nutritionists understand the unique challenges of eating during cancer treatment and provide practical, compassionate support.

Meet Our Nutritionists

Pain and Symptom Management

Pain and discomfort shouldn't define your cancer journey. Our specialized team helps manage cancer-related symptoms, improving your day-to-day quality of life.

Our approach includes:

  • Comprehensive assessment of pain patterns and triggers
  • Personalized pain management plan combining medications and non-drug approaches
  • Regular monitoring and adjustment of pain control strategies
  • Home-based techniques family members can help implement
  • Coordination with your primary oncologist on symptom management
  • Emergency response protocols for breakthrough pain

We believe effective pain management is not just about medications, but about finding the right combination of approaches for your unique situation.

Get Relief Now

Emotional and Psychological Support

Cancer affects not just the body, but the mind and spirit. Our mental health team provides compassionate support through every emotional challenge the cancer journey brings.

Support services include:

  • One-on-one counseling sessions with psycho-oncologists
  • Family support to help loved ones cope and provide effective support
  • Techniques for managing anxiety, depression, and fear
  • Guided meditation and relaxation practices
  • Connection to peer support communities
  • Survivorship counseling during and after treatment

Our sessions are conducted in the comfort and privacy of your home through secure video calls or phone conversations.

Begin Your Healing Journey

Professional Home Care

Some aspects of cancer care are best delivered at home. Our skilled nurses and healthcare workers bring hospital-quality care to you, reducing hospital visits and increasing comfort.

Our home care options include:

  • Skilled nursing visits for medication administration and monitoring
  • Post-surgical wound care and recovery support
  • Intravenous therapy administration
  • Port and central line maintenance
  • Symptom monitoring and management
  • Basic physical therapy and mobility assistance
  • Family caregiver training and support

All our home care professionals are specially trained in oncology care and follow strict infection control protocols to ensure your safety.

Arrange Home Care

Chemotherapy Services

Receiving chemotherapy shouldn't always mean long journeys and hospital stays. We offer flexible options for chemotherapy administration that consider your comfort and circumstances.

Day Care Chemotherapy:

  • Comfortable treatment spaces with privacy options
  • Administration by experienced oncology nurses
  • Complete monitoring throughout your session
  • Pre-medications to minimize side effects
  • Nutritional support during longer sessions
  • Entertainment options to help pass the time
  • Transportation assistance when needed

Home Chemotherapy (for eligible treatments):

  • Safety assessment of home environment
  • Administration by specialized oncology nurses
  • Remote supervision by oncologists
  • All necessary equipment and supplies provided
  • Careful waste disposal and safety protocols
  • Family education on post-treatment care

Your oncologist will help determine which setting is most appropriate for your specific treatment.

Learn About Your Options

Second Opinion Service

Important decisions deserve careful consideration. Our second opinion service connects you with experienced specialists who review your case and offer fresh insights.

The process includes:

  • Thorough review of your medical records and test results
  • Fresh interpretation of imaging and pathology findings
  • Consultation with specialists in your specific type of cancer
  • Discussion of treatment alternatives you may not have considered
  • Identification of clinical trial opportunities if appropriate
  • Comprehensive written report of findings and recommendations

A second opinion can provide peace of mind about your current treatment plan or open doors to new approaches worth considering.
