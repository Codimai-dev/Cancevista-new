Our Story

In India, cancer care presents a stark divide. While urban centers offer world-class treatment, millions in smaller cities and rural areas face impossible choices – traveling vast distances, spending life savings, or worse, abandoning treatment altogether.

This reality touched our founding team deeply. They witnessed countless families struggling to access quality cancer care outside major cities – parents selling land, elderly patients discontinuing therapy, and breadwinners forced to choose between treatment and survival.

Cancervistas was born from a simple yet powerful belief: geography and financial constraints shouldn’t determine cancer outcomes. Everyone deserves quality care that respects their dignity, circumstances, and unique needs.

Today, we’re building bridges across the cancer care gap – connecting patients with specialists, bringing treatments closer to home, and ensuring that quality care reaches every corner of India.

The Cancer Care Gap: India’s Reality

  • 13 lakh+ new cancer cases annually
  • Only 27% of districts have specialized centers
  • 78% of advanced centers in metros
  • 70% of population lives outside metros
  • ₹3–7 lakhs avg. out-of-pocket expenses
  • 60% patients travel over 100 km for treatment
  • 1:2000 oncologist-to-patient ratio

Behind each of these statistics are real families facing real challenges. At Cancervistas, we see the humans behind the numbers.
