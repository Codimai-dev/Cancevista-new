Privacy Policy - Cancervistas

At Cancervistas, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, use our services, or engage with us online or offline. We operate in compliance with all applicable data protection laws and regulations in India.

Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: We collect your name, email address, phone number, and address when you register, request a service, or contact us.
  • Payment Information: We collect billing details such as your name, billing address, and payment method. We do not store or process credit card details; payments are securely handled through authorized third-party payment gateways.
  • Health and Service Information: We may collect limited health-related information necessary to facilitate your requested cancer care services (e.g., consultations, diagnostics, or treatment coordination). All health data is handled with utmost confidentiality.
  • Usage and Preference Information: We collect information on how you use our website and services, including preferences, through analytics tools like Google Analytics and cookies.
  • Transaction Information: We record details of your transactions, including services availed, costs, and related communications.
  • Technical Data: We collect IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and browsing activity for security and analytics purposes.

How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you: When you fill forms, contact us, or use our services.
  • Automatically: Through cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies.
  • From third parties: Including partner hospitals, diagnostic centers, or affiliates who help deliver services.

Use of Your Information

  • To provide, coordinate, and manage cancer care services including consultations, diagnostics, and treatments.
  • To improve our platform, services, and user experience.
  • To process transactions and send related communications.
  • To share updates, offers, or awareness information (only with your consent).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Sharing of Your Information

  • With healthcare providers, diagnostic partners, and service affiliates for seamless care delivery.
  • With third-party vendors for operational, analytics, and marketing support.
  • When required by law or government authorities.
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes.

Your Consent

By using the Cancervistas website or services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this policy.

Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, analyze site usage, and display relevant content. You can modify your browser settings to disable cookies; however, some parts of the website may not function properly.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Email Opt-Out

You may opt out of promotional or marketing emails by contacting us at unsubscribe@cancervistas.com.

Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. Cancervistas is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of such third-party sites.
